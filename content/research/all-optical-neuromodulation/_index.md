### Overview
This line of work explores architectures for optical brain–computer interfaces that close the loop between optical readout and optical stimulation. Research spans modeling, simulation, and prototype control strategies aimed at enabling precise, adaptive neuromodulation.

### Key projects
- NSFC project: "Intelligent Frameworks for All‑optical Closed‑loop Neuromodulation" (PI, 2026–2028).
- Simulation and prototype integration of optical readout with learned controllers.

### Representative outputs
- Project proposals, technical reports, and workshop presentations on closed‑loop optical control strategies.
